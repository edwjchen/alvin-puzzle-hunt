üí° Explanation

Clue 1: "I speak without a mouth" - This suggests something that makes sound but doesn't have a physical mouth.

Clue 2: "I hear without ears" - This suggests something that responds to sound but doesn't have physical ears.

Clue 3: "I have no body, but come alive with wind" - This suggests something that is not a living being but becomes active or makes sound when wind passes through it.


Answer: ECHO

Reasoning: An echo "speaks" (repeats sound) without a mouth, "hears" (responds to sound) without ears, and "comes alive" when wind or sound waves pass through it, creating the echo effect.

üéØ The Solution

The answer is ECHO.

An echo speaks without a mouth (it repeats sounds), hears without ears (it responds to sound waves), and comes alive with wind (sound waves created by wind or other sources).

Think about things that make sounds when wind passes through them. What repeats sounds without having a mouth?
